Platform Capabilities

Built for regulated industries. Trusted by the teams who run them.

From asset registers and scheduled maintenance to role-based access controls and SLA monitoring; Artemis gives every team the tools to work with confidence and the audit trail to prove it.

Asset Registers & Maintenance Planning

Build your asset register. Create your work bank. Track everything through.

Artemis gives you complete control of your asset register; from individual items to complex multi-site hierarchies. Build structured maintenance work banks with scheduled checks, assign standards and compliance requirements, and track every task through to completion with a full, defensible audit trail.

  • Comprehensive asset register with configurable attributes, hierarchy, and location mapping
  • Build maintenance work banks with recurring checks, schedules, and compliance standards
  • Track assets via QR code, RFID, or asset number; full lifecycle history retained
Scheduled & Reactive Job Management

One platform for every job. Planned or reactive.

Create job cards for both scheduled preventive maintenance and reactive repairs from a single platform. Assign to technicians, set priorities and due dates, attach documentation and checklists, and track progress in real time from creation through to sign-off and close-out.

  • Job cards for planned preventive maintenance with automated scheduling triggers
  • Reactive job creation from mobile app, QR code, RFID tag, or API integration
  • Priority-based work queues with real-time status tracking and full technician assignment
Role-Based Controls & Permissions

The right access for the right people. Without the bottleneck.

Artemis gives you granular, role-based permission controls so every team member has exactly the access they need; without waiting for a system administrator. Fleet officers can update mileage, brake condition, and tyre data independently. Engineers log faults and close jobs. Managers approve, reject, and re-assign; all within the boundaries you define and the audit trail you need.

  • Granular role-based permissions configured per user, team, and asset type
  • Self-service data entry for field teams; no admin bottleneck on routine updates
  • Hierarchy controls for approval, rejection, re-testing, and kit change sign-off
Built for Every Team

The right tools for every team
that keeps operations running

Whether you're allocating work to field teams, reporting a fault from the job, or tracking SLA performance across contractors; Artemis surfaces the right view for the right person.

For Operations Managers

Allocate and assign work at the click of a button

See technician availability, current workload, and skill sets across your operation; then allocate and assign jobs instantly. The right resource, on the right job, at the right time.

For Field Teams & Asset Users

Report issues instantly; from the app, QR code, or RFID tag

Anyone in the organisation can raise a reactive job in seconds; by scanning a QR code on the asset, tapping an RFID tag, using the mobile app, or via API. Issues reach the right team immediately with no paper, no email chains, and no delay.

For Finance & Compliance Teams

Monitor SLA performance and track asset value over time

Track contractor SLA compliance, monitor response and resolution times against targets, and follow asset values and depreciation across the estate. The financial and compliance intelligence that keeps procurement decisions evidence-based and auditable.
