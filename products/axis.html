Root Cause Analysis

Understand exactly why.
Not just that something failed.

When Axis detects an anomaly, it automatically generates a root cause report — analysing contributing factors, identifying the primary failure mechanism, and recommending the specific corrective action needed.

  • AI-generated root cause reports with confidence ratings per diagnosis
  • Contributing factor breakdown — age, load patterns, environmental data
  • Recommended actions mapped directly to maintenance work orders

Axis is designed for operations that cannot afford unplanned failures — where asset reliability, maintenance efficiency, and operational continuity matter.

By combining real-time sensor data, machine learning, automated diagnostics, and smart alerting, Axis turns reactive maintenance into a proactive, evidence-led programme. Teams act earlier, engineers focus on what matters, and stakeholders have clear visibility of asset health across the estate.

This helps organisations reduce unplanned downtime, extend asset life, and demonstrate that maintenance decisions are data-driven, proportionate, and defensible.
